Frequently Asked Questions

How can I play Skwiz?

Skwiz is a collaborative drawing app based on the classic pen and paper game, exquisite corpse. Coming soon to the Apple's app store.

What's jERKS iNC?

jERKS iNC is a studio created by James Dybvig, Vince Joy, and Michael McCombie for creating animated and interactive digital content.

How do I add a link to my profile?

Anyone with a Skwiz Pro account can add a website link to their profile. After updating to a Pro account, navigate to your profile page anc click the link icon in the top right corner.
